Abstract:

We report computations of the production cross-section of longitudinal electroweak and Higgs boson pairs within Effective Field Theory for the Electroweak sector (including the Higgs). We are now focusing on gauge boson-Higgs boson two-body axial-vector resonances and show a typical cross section.
