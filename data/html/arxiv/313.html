4.1 Star formation and abundances

The young starburst inferred by the detections of high ionization emission line of HeIIÂ Î»ğœ†\lambda4686 and the blue WR bump in this work and previous works (Guseva etÂ al., 2000; Brinchmann etÂ al., 2008) is confirmed by the age estimates made here for the bright and faint regions in Mrk 22 as âˆ¼similar-to\sim 4 Myr and âˆ¼similar-to\sim 10 Myr respectively. The winds from the most massive stars and the high rate of supernovae in the young star-bursting region are expected to enrich the ISM.
