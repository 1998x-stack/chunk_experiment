Abstract

We derive in this article the exact norm in the Grand Lebesgue Spaces (GLS) estimates for Fourier transform acting on the functions defined in the infinite local compact Abelian (LCA) group, compact or discrete.

Key words and phrases: Local compact Abelian (LCA) group, dual group and dual Haar measures, distance, Fourier transform, Lebesgue - Riesz and Grand Lebesgue Spaces (GLS), fundamental functions, compactness, discreetness, rearrangement invariant space, exponential Orlicz spaces, natural function.
