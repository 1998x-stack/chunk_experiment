Abstract

I Introduction

Ever since its inception, the standard model (SM) of elementary particle physics has been extremely successful with its predictions tested to high precision. However, it is well known that the SM is not complete, as it does not describe gravity or dark matter, for example. Current approaches to establish a comprehensive and fundamental theory allow for deviations from exact Lorentz symmetry.
